Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade between Free, Pro, and Enterprise whenever you like. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I go over my included API calls?

On the Free plan, requests are capped at 1,000 calls per month. On Pro and Enterprise, additional calls beyond your included quota are billed at €10 per 1,000 calls.

Is self-hosting available outside Enterprise?

Self-hosted, on-premise deployment is currently an Enterprise-only feature, alongside SSO (SAML/OIDC) and SOC-2 compliance.

How does Enterprise pricing scale?

Enterprise pricing ranges from €30 per user per month down to €2.50 per user per month as your seat count grows. Contact sales for a quote tailored to your team.

What is private repo parsing used for?

Private repo parsing tokens cover indexing and analysis of private repository content, billed at €25 per 1 million tokens on Pro and Enterprise plans.
