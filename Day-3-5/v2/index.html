Frequently Asked Questions

Plans & Pricing
What's included in the Basic plan?

The Basic plan includes Management Console, EU VAT Support, MultiFeature, Subscription, TryAndBuy, Maintenance, PayPal/Stripe payments, RESTful API, and NetLicensing Shop.

What's the difference between Basic and Premium?

Premium includes everything in Basic plus Product Usage Tracking, Floating and NodeLocked licensing models, Shop Customization, Customer Portal, more Licensees (500 vs 100), more Products (10 vs 3), more API Requests (50 vs 10), and Online Licensing Training.

Can I upgrade from Basic to Premium?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. The price difference will be prorated for the remainder of your billing period.

Licensing Models
Which licensing models are included?

Basic includes MultiFeature, Subscription, and TryAndBuy. Premium adds Floating and NodeLocked on top of those.

What is PayPerUse, Quota, Rental, PricingTable, and Discount?

These licensing models are available as add-ons. Contact sales for pricing and availability.

Support
What support is included?

Both plans include Standard Technical Support. Priority Technical Support and a Dedicated Technical Success Manager are available as add-ons.
