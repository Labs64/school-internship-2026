Usage & Limits

Pricing & Plans
Free gives you access to public repositories, basic access control, OAuth 2.0 sign-in, and 1,000 API calls every month — no private repos or team features, but plenty for solo, public-facing work.
Pro is €10 per seat per month, with one seat per teamspace member. Each seat includes 5,000 API calls; beyond that you pay €10 per extra 1,000 calls. Private repository parsing is billed separately at €25 per 1M tokens.
Everything in Pro, plus SOC-2 compliance, SAML/OIDC SSO, a self-hosted deployment option, and dedicated support — priced per seat with volume discounts as your team grows.
Enterprise
Enterprise pricing scales with team size: smaller teams start around €30 per user per month, while larger organizations can pay as little as €2.50 per user per month. Annual commitments may unlock further discounts — reach out to our sales team for an exact quote.
Yes. Enterprise customers can run the platform entirely within their own infrastructure. Contact our team to scope a self-hosted deployment for your organization.
